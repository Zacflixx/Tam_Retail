Managed Services

TAMAWAL Managed Services are complete management and control of TAMMWEL decision platform, including operation, maintenance and optimization. By presenting the solution as a managed cloud service provider, a business can ensure provided services run efficiently. Outsourcing fully efficient solution management also allows businesses to avoid new hiring and external development costs.

TAMAWAL Managed Services can provide private, public and hybrid integrations with its solutions and services over cloud. Working with a financing managed services provider is a collaborative process.
