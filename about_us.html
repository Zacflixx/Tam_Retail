About Us

We are Tamawal

Tamawal® is an innovative fintech platform that works as an aggregator for the financial sector to link finance requesters with banks and financial institutions as a Digital Financing Broker (DFB). Tamawal® extends its services to provide a variety of services to serve retail and corporate financing clients. Instead of going to each and every finance provider to find better deals, customers visit one platform (Tamawal®) to explore all products that are available from finance providers that match their needs and credit scores to save the time and effort of all customers. Tamawal® is aimed at promoting the convenient banking culture and reducing the efforts of the clients by bringing the financing services digitally at their hands which saves a lot of their time and efforts.
